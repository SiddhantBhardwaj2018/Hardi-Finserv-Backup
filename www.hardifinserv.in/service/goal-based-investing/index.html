Goal Based Investing

Goal Based Investing

A relatively new approach to wealth management that emphasizes investing with the objective of attaining specific life goals.

Goal-based investing (GBI) involves a wealth manager or investment firms clients measuring their progress towards the specific life goals such as saving for childrens education or building a retirement nest-egg, rather than focusing on generating the highest possible portfolio return or beating the market.
